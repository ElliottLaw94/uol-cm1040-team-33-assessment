About Us

Pixel Park is a Theme Park based off of nostalgic retro video games. Our aim is to combine gaming and thrills that anyone in the family can enjoy! Our park is tailored to suit the needs of families, children, teens and adults alike! We offer a range of thrill rides for the adrenaline junkies, paired with slower paced rides for the younger gamers and families. Founded in 2024, we offer a modern take on introducing retro video games to newer generations whilst keeping the thrilling and fun aspects of a Theme Park.
