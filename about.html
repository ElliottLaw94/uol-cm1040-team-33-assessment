About Us


Welcome to Pixel Park, where the nostalgia of retro video games meets the excitement of a modern theme park! Our mission is to blend the thrill of gaming with heart-pounding rides, creating an unforgettable experience for every member of the family.

At Pixel Park, we cater to guests of all ages-from the youngest gamers to teens, adults, and everyone in between. For the adrenaline junkies, we offer a variety of exhilarating rides that will leave you breathless. Meanwhile, our gentler attractions provide fun and adventure for younger visitors and families looking for a more relaxed pace.

Founded in 2024, Pixel Park introduces the charm of classic video games to a new generation, all while preserving the excitement and fun that define a top-tier theme park. Join us for a unique journey where gaming nostalgia and thrilling adventures come together to create memories that last a lifetime!

How to get here

By Car

Please use the postcode: CV3 6PS on your satnav. Our theme park is closest to the A46.

By Train

You can travel via train to Coventry Train Station (COV). There will be a shuttle bus every hour that starts from the bus station and travels to the train station, that goes directly to the theme park. It costs £2 a trip.

By Bus

Please look in your local area for coaches to Coventry Bus Station. From there, you can get the Theme Park shuttle bus that will take you directly to the Theme Park. It costs £2 a trip.
