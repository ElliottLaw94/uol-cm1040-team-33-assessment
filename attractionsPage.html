Introduction to main attractions


    Pixel Pendulum: Step aboard and prepare for an epic journey through the digital realm! Swing back and forth on this thrilling ride, feeling the rush of pixelated excitement as you soar through the air. Height requirement: 110 cm. Age requirement: 8 years old and above.


    Super Sonic Dash: Feel the rush as you race through loops and corkscrews at supersonic speeds in this roller coaster themed after everyone's favorite blue hedgehog. Height requirement: 110cm. Weight limit: 120kg. Age requirement: 8 years old and above.


    Space Invasion: Climb aboard your own space cruiser and gear up for an adrenaline-pumping laser shooting experience! Join forces with fellow recruits as you embark on a mission to defend Earth against waves of pixelated alien invaders. Take aim, fire lasers, and rack up points as you navigate through cosmic battlegrounds in this thrilling cart-based ride. Height requirement: 100 cm. Age requirement: 6 years old and above.

Our Other Attractions


    Super Splash Bros: Brave treacherous waters, dodge obstacles, and face off against familiar foes in a quest for victory. Height requirement: 110 cm. Age requirement: 8 years old and above.


    Tetris Tower Drop: Stack, rotate, and clear falling blocks before taking a thrilling plunge down a towering Tetris tower. Height requirement: 107 cm. Weight limit: 136 kg.


    Pac-Man Panic: Navigate through a maze filled with twists, turns, and ghosts in this interactive ride inspired by the iconic video game. Height requirement: 110 cm. Weight limit: 120 kg. Age requirement: 8 years old and above.


    Mario's Mushroom Spin: Join Mario and friends for a whimsical tea cup ride through the colorful Mushroom Kingdom! Spin, twirl, and giggle your way through this delightful attraction inspired by the beloved Super Mario games. Height requirement: 90 cm. Age requirement: 4 years old and above.


    Kirby's Dreamland Float: Drift through a whimsical world filled with floating islands and colourful characters in this gentle, family-friendly boat ride. Height requirement: 91 cm. Age requirement: 4 years old and above.


    Kong's Coaster Chaos: Are you ready for an adventure through the jungle? Zoom through twists and turns, dodge barrels, and swing through vines as you race to the finish line in this thrilling ride inspired by the classic Donkey Kong games. Height requirement: 120 cm. Weight limit: 120 kg. Age requirement: 10 years old and above.

Disclaimer: Participation in amusement park rides may pose risks to individuals with certain medical conditions. Guests should consult a physician before riding if they have heart problems, high blood pressure, back or neck injuries, motion sickness, or are pregnant. Follow all safety guidelines provided by ride operators, and use caution if you have any health concerns.
